Mommy Map is an inventory of family-friendly services and businesses in the Greater Vancouver and Santa Fe areas. While we do not collect personal information directly from you unless you choose to provide it, we do process certain types of information to provide our services, understand our audience, and improve the app.

This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors and users (“Users”) of the Mommy Map mobile application (“App”).

Usage Information We Collect

  • Location and Map Usage Data: Mommy Map uses Mapbox to display locations on a map. By default, Mapbox collects anonymized location and map usage information (“Telemetry”). You can opt out at any time by clicking the Mapbox logo in the app and selecting “Telemetry”. We do not collect precise GPS location for any other purpose. We may determine your approximate country and city location based on your IP address.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized information such as page views, visits, referring pages, search queries, and actions taken within the app.
  • Device Data: We may collect device-related information such as device model, operating system, app version, performance metrics, and crash/error logs to improve the user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and provide core features of the App.
  • To fix errors and troubleshoot technical issues quickly.
  • To improve usability, performance, and content relevance.

Transmission to Data Processors

We share information with trusted service providers (“Data Processors”) who help us operate the App:

  • Mapbox: As our map provider, Mapbox uses anonymized telemetry data to improve its map services. You can opt out of telemetry in the App at any time by clicking the Mapbox logo and selecting “Telemetry”.
